Oil lamp

Growing up, every house had one these lamps . Power outages were a part of our childhood. The work in the house had to stop. We all would gather around the light, warm and snug. The smell of oil was tranquil and happy. The comfort of having electricity could not beat the serenity of living without it. Who know electricity would be so vital to my future career?
