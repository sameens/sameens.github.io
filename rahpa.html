Rahpa

Rahpa is a startup that provides smart transportation solutions.

Their existing product, Panda, allowed parents and school admins to track students during their commute via school provided vehicles. I redesigned their native apps (for parents and drivers) and web product (used by school admins).

I also designed the initial flow of their new product which allowed companies to keep track and manage their field staff.
